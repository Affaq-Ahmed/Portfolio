## Overview

Divorce Stoppers AI is an AI-based relationship-coaching platform designed to
improve communication and reduce divorce risk — helping people fix their texts
and conversations using proven coaching methods, delivered through AI.

## My Role & Contributions

I built the AI core of the product:

- **Complete RAG system** — designed and built the full retrieval-augmented
  generation pipeline.
- **Vector database** — implemented vector storage and search.
- **Embedding pipelines** — built the pipelines that turn content into
  embeddings.
- **OCR integration** — so users can upload screenshots of conversations to be
  read and analysed.
- **LLM integrations** — Claude and OpenAI.
- **Payments** — PayPal integration.

## Key Features

- **AI relationship coaching** grounded in a proven method via RAG.
- **Message analysis** — upload texts (including screenshots via OCR) for advice.
- **Vector search** over coaching knowledge.
- **Payments** through PayPal.

## Tech Stack & Architecture

- **App:** Next.js with Supabase.
- **AI:** Claude and OpenAI, with OpenAI embeddings and a vector database for
  retrieval.
- **Input:** OCR for ingesting screenshots of conversations.
- **Payments:** PayPal.

## Challenges & Solutions

- **A full RAG pipeline** — embeddings, vector storage, and retrieval so the
  coaching draws on the right material rather than generic advice.
- **OCR ingestion** — letting users upload screenshots of their conversations
  and reliably extracting the text for analysis.
