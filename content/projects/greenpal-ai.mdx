## Overview

An AI-powered outsourcing and lead-generation tool built for **GreenPal** (the
US lawn-care service). It automates prospecting and outreach — discovering leads
and reaching out to them — using AI-driven workflows on top of several data
sources.

## My Role & Contributions

I led this project as **Tech Lead**, owning its direction and delivery rather
than its hands-on implementation. Under my lead, the team built:

- **AI-powered outreach automation** — Claude-driven workflows that handle
  outreach end to end.
- **Lead discovery** — finding and enriching prospects.
- **Integrations** — **Apollo** and **RocketReach** for lead data, and the
  **Gmail API** for sending outreach.
- **Website scraping** — gathering additional lead information from the web.

## Key Features

- **Automated outreach** powered by Claude.
- **Lead discovery & enrichment** via Apollo and RocketReach.
- **Website scraping** for extra context on prospects.
- **Gmail-based outreach** sending.

## Tech Stack & Architecture

- **Backend:** Ruby on Rails.
- **AI:** Claude API for the outreach workflows.
- **Data sources:** Apollo API, RocketReach, and website scraping.
- **Email:** Gmail API.

## Challenges & Solutions

- **Unifying messy lead data** — orchestrating Apollo, RocketReach, and scraped
  web data into clean, usable leads.
- **AI-driven outreach** — Claude-powered workflows that automate prospecting and
  messaging while staying relevant.
