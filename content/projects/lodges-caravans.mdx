## Overview

Lodges & Caravans For Sale is a UK-based SaaS marketplace for buying and selling
holiday homes and caravans. It lets people buy directly from private owners
across the UK — no agents, no cuts, no inflated prices — positioned as the UK's
only marketplace dedicated to lodges and holiday caravans.

## My Role & Contributions

I worked across the frontend and backend:

- **Map-based property search** — find listings by location on an interactive
  map.
- **Property listing management** — create and manage listings.
- **Chat-based inquiry system** — buyers and owners connect directly in-platform.
- **OTM integration** — integrated the third-party OTM feed.
- **Payment integration** and **UI implementation**.

## Key Features

- **Map search** for properties by location.
- **Listings** managed by private owners.
- **In-platform chat** between buyers and sellers.
- **Payments** via Stripe.
- **OTM integration** for listings data.

## Tech Stack & Architecture

- **Frontend:** Next.js / React.
- **Backend:** Node.js with MongoDB.
- **Payments:** Stripe.
- **Location:** Google Maps for map-based search.
- **Integration:** OTM.

## Challenges & Solutions

- **Map-based discovery** — building responsive, location-driven search over the
  listings with Google Maps.
- **Third-party data** — integrating the OTM feed so listings stay consistent.
- **Direct buyer–seller contact** — a chat-based inquiry system that keeps
  conversations in-platform.
