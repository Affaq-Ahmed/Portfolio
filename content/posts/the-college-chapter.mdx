After finishing high school at **Fauji Foundation School, Khushab**, I found myself waiting eagerly for my 10th class results.  
While waiting, I joined a **summer camp at Superior College (Jauharabad Campus)** for *FSc Pre-Engineering*.  

When the results finally came, I scored **89% overall**, with **92% in 10th grade** — missing the *90% mark* by just **1%**.  
That single percent meant missing out on the **Punjab Government laptop scholarship**, which was given to students scoring 90% or above.  
It stung, but it also taught me early on that sometimes even small margins matter — in academics, sports, and life.

## The Scholarship Dilemma

Superior College offered me a **full-fee scholarship** — a generous opportunity by all means.  
But my heart pulled me back to **Fauji Foundation College**, the place where I’d already spent 12 years studying, growing, and building memories.  
Even though Fauji Foundation offered only a **75% fee waiver**, I chose loyalty over convenience.

That decision wasn’t just about academics — it was about **belonging**.  
It was where I had friends, teachers who knew me, and a deep sense of comfort that only comes from being home.

## Friendship and Growth

I convinced my best friend, **Zakria Rehman**, to join me at Fauji Foundation — though he went for *Pre-Medical* while I pursued *Pre-Engineering*.  
He’s now a **Veterinary Doctor**, and our paths — though different — began in the same classrooms.

During those two years, I learned a lot — academically and personally.  
I scored **85% in my first year** and **83% overall in Intermediate (Pre-Engineering)**.  
I played sports, built friendships, and started seeing what kind of person I wanted to become.

## The Turning Point

It was during this time that I set my eyes on **Software Engineering**.  
That became my focus — my *goal*.  
Fauji Foundation gave me direction, discipline, and the spark that led me toward technology.  

And somewhere in those same corridors, I also saw for the first time the girl who would later become my **life partner** — another reason that makes this place unforgettable.

## A Place That Built Me

When I look back, **Fauji Foundation** isn’t just a name on my certificates.  
It’s where I grew up, where I learned to stay loyal, where I made lifelong friends, and where I discovered who I wanted to be.

Spending almost **14 years** in the same institution says something — about **consistency**, **loyalty**, and **passion**.  
It’s a chapter I’ll always cherish — because it built the foundation of who I am today.
